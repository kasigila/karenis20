About BYZ in the Hood

We are more than just an event promoter - we are curators of experiences.

Creating Unforgettable Nightlife Experiences Since 2018

BYZ in the Hood is Dar es Salaam's premier event and nightlife brand, dedicated to creating high-energy social experiences that bring people together through music and community.

From intimate apartment sessions to large-scale beach parties and club nights, we've built a reputation for delivering exceptional events that capture the vibrant spirit of Dar es Salaam's nightlife.

Our events feature a diverse mix of music styles - Afro, Reggae, Dancehall, Caribbean, and worldwide hits - ensuring there's something for everyone on the dance floor.
